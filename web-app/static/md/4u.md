<h2 is="e-h" data-color="primary">How I work</h2>
<span is="e-caption" data-color="muted">What you get when we collaborate — and a few principles I won't bend on</span>

<div is="e-grid" data-columns="3" data-gap="lg" data-margin-top="md" data-width="full">

<div is="e-card" data-width="full" data-padding="lg">
<h3 is="e-h" data-font-size="lg">Zero bloat</h3>
<p data-font-size="sm">Lean architecture — fast to ship, cheap to run. Every technical choice serves the product and the bottom line.</p>
</div>

<div is="e-card" data-width="full" data-padding="lg">
<h3 is="e-h" data-font-size="lg">Solo execution</h3>
<p data-font-size="sm">Architect, implement, deploy — often alone or with a tiny team. Production-ready MVPs in months, not quarters.</p>
</div>

<div is="e-card" data-width="full" data-padding="lg">
<h3 is="e-h" data-font-size="lg">One focus</h3>
<p data-font-size="sm">One exclusive engagement at a time. Direct communication, zero-fluff discovery, then working software.</p>
</div>

<div is="e-card" data-width="full" data-padding="lg">
<h3 is="e-h" data-font-size="lg">If it works, don't rewrite it</h3>
<p data-font-size="sm">When performance and security are fine, we don't rebuild because a new language or library version came out. Upgrades happen when there's a real payoff — not on autopilot.</p>
</div>

<div is="e-card" data-width="full" data-padding="lg">
<h3 is="e-h" data-font-size="lg">No microservices. No SaaS sprawl</h3>
<p data-font-size="sm">One coherent system beats twenty services that multiply cost and ops. And if something takes thirty lines of code, we don't add another monthly subscription.</p>
</div>

<div is="e-card" data-width="full" data-padding="lg">
<h3 is="e-h" data-font-size="lg">Test what users actually do</h3>
<p data-font-size="sm">End-to-end tests on real product flows — not mock-heavy unit tests that pass while production breaks. We verify features work, not that fake data behaved.</p>
</div>

<div is="e-card" data-width="full" data-padding="lg">
<h3 is="e-h" data-font-size="lg">Real problems only</h3>
<p data-font-size="sm">We don't invent complexity because it's fashionable — replacing a straightforward solution with layers of abstraction, hooks, and ceremony that slow delivery and burn budget.</p>
</div>

<div is="e-card" data-width="full" data-padding="lg">
<h3 is="e-h" data-font-size="lg">Ship fast, change fast</h3>
<p data-font-size="sm">I prefer stacks where iteration stays quick as the codebase grows — no hour-long builds between a fix and seeing it live. Speed of change is a business feature.</p>
</div>

<div is="e-card" data-width="full" data-padding="lg">
<h3 is="e-h" data-font-size="lg">Explicit over clever</h3>
<p data-font-size="sm">Code should say what the program does — direct, readable, no magic. Easier to maintain, easier to trust, cheaper to hand off or extend later.</p>
</div>
